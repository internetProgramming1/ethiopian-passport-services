New Passport Appointment

First time applicants are requested to schedule for appointment. You receive confirmation via email or SMS. Print the last page which has your appointment date and time. Take the paper with you to your appointment consular.

Renewal Passport

Expired, damaged or page runout (not expired) passport, applicants are requested to schedule for appointment. You receive confirmation via email or SMS. Print the last page which has your appointment date and time. Take the paper with you to your appointment consular.

Lost Passport

Lost / Stolen passport, applicants are requested to schedule for appointment. You receive confirmation via email or SMS. Print the last page which has your appointment date and time. Take the paper with you to your appointment consular
