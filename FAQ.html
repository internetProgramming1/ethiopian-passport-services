Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for an Ethiopian passport?

You can apply for an Ethiopian passport online through the official website or visit the nearest embassy.

What documents are required for the application?

You will need a birth certificate, identification card, and passport-sized photographs.

How long does it take to process the application?

The processing time can vary, but it typically takes between 2 to 6 weeks.

Can I track my application status online?

Yes, you can track your application status on the Ethiopian passport service website.

What is the fee for applying for a passport?

The fee varies based on the type of passport and the processing speed you choose. Please check the official website for the latest fees.

What if I need to travel urgently and my passport is expired?

In such cases, you should apply for a passport renewal as soon as possible. If you have urgent travel plans, contact the embassy for possible expedited options.

Can I apply for a passport if I am outside Ethiopia?

Yes, Ethiopian citizens abroad can apply for a passport at their nearest embassy or consulate.

What should I do if I lose my passport?

Report the loss to the local police and then apply for a replacement passport at your nearest embassy or consulate.

Can I renew my passport online?

Yes, passport renewals can typically be completed online. Check the official website for specific instructions.

Is there an expedited processing option for passport applications?

Yes, expedited processing may be available for an additional fee. Check the official website for details on how to apply.

What should I do if my name has changed since my last passport?

If your name has changed, you must provide legal documentation of the name change when applying for a new passport.

Are there any age restrictions for applying for a passport?

There are no specific age restrictions. Children can apply for a passport, but a parent or guardian must sign the application.

What if my passport application is denied?

You will receive a notification explaining the reasons for denial. You may have the option to appeal the decision.
